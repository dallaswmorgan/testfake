Directions

  1. Assemble chocolate on half cracker in preparation for mallow
  2. Toast marshmallow to taste. I like mine golden brown with gooey insides!
  3. Gently place toasted marshmallow on chocolate
  4. Pinch other half of cracker on top of mallow
  5. Enjoy!
