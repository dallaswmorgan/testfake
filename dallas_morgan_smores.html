Description

S'mores have been a staple of campfires since the dawn of time. Ever since Man discovered fire, we have been perfecting the art of the S'more. Wikipidea History

Ingredients

  1. Graham Crackers
  2. Marshmallows
  3. Chocolate Bar

Directions

  1. Assemble chocolate on half cracker in preparation for mallow
  2. Toast marshmallow to taste. I like mine golden brown with gooey insides!
  3. Gently place toasted marshmallow on chocolate
  4. Pinch other half of cracker on top of mallow
  5. Enjoy!
